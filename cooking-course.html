Course Program


Main themes addressed


Knife Skills


Proper professional knife use


This topic will cover the basic knife use, its maintanance on a daily basis, how to keep your tool always sharp and ready to use for every necessities and how to undestand which knife suits you at its best at the shape of your hand.


Different knives kind


In this argument I will show the different shape of knife that you can use in a professional kitchen enviroment and how these knives can help you, if used for a certain kind of preparation, to reach the best result.



Cooking techniques


Kind of cooking fats


This topic will cover the different properties that fats in the cooking procedure add to the dishes and which is better use in a certain preparation.


Materials for utensils


In this argument we will go through the materials used to fabricate pan and pot and why also the choice of this detail is essential to reach the optimal outcome for every cooking technique of the wide range possible.


Ingredients


Seasonality


This topic will cover the Importance of seasonality in the choice of ingredients obtaining the best natural taste from each ingredients making a top quality dish having the chance to work with fresh raw materials having the opportunity to reduce the enviromental foot print too.


Build up a menu


In this argument we will go through the structure of a traditional menu and the right order of service for each dish building up menu for weddings, different events and a tasting menu for a gourmet restaurant.
